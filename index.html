About Me

I'm Abdalaziz Esam, a creative and detail-oriented Front-End & Mobile Developer based in Cairo, Egypt. I specialize in building responsive, user-centered applications using Flutter, React, Angular, and other modern technologies. With a strong foundation in both design and development, I’m passionate about turning ideas into smooth, interactive digital experiences. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and constantly push myself to learn, adapt, and stay up to date with the latest tools, frameworks, and UI trends. This blend of development and storytelling allows me to connect with a wider audience and bring a unique creative perspective to everything I build. When I’m not coding or creating content, you’ll find me exploring new design ideas, leveling up my editing skills, or diving into the next exciting challenge.
